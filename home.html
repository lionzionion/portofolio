My story

I grew up in a family that combined architects and musicians, and my growing up environment was permeated with the synergy between engineering and architecture, where I learned to value teamwork. Many challenges were overcome collectively. When I became an adult, I chose to pursue a career as a civil engineer. During the pandemic, with the extra time at home, I became interested in developing applications, games and web pages. Today, I have a year of experience in various projects, in addition to having completed several courses and obtained certificates. I am fully aware that technology is a tool created and used by people. With this in mind, my approach focuses on humanizing the process of designing and developing technological solutions. I firmly believe that empathy and understanding users' needs and challenges are fundamental to creating products and services that truly add value.
